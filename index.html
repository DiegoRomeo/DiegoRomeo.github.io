Maker Faire Rome 2021

2021-10-09 :: [DiegoRomeo]
#posts  #Event  #MakerFaireRome 
Maker Faire Rome is the biggest edition worldwide of Maker Faire, a science faire for makers of any age. I had an amazing time being an exhibitor at the IX edition (2021). Hundreds of innovative projects... It was like a playground for a child! For this science faire I made 11 classical physics simulators in Python, to support teaching in the pandemic era: the idea of building the simulators came from the fact that in our school we were not able to use our labs to make scientific experiments, due to COVID-19. So I decided to make this set of simulators that are accessible through an UI interface (which was also designed by me using Adobe Illustrator). The user can also read through the Python App the theory behind each simulator.
